Investment thesis

Three investment themes driving the next phase of the Canadian cannabis market.

01

Infrastructure and compliance tech

Software companies solving regulatory complexity for licensed producers, retailers, and distributors. Seed-to-sale tracking, licence management, excise tax automation, and regulatory reporting tools that reduce compliance headcount costs. Every LP in Canada is a potential customer and the problem does not go away with scale.

02

Retail and consumer platforms

Cannabis retail technology, consumer discovery platforms, loyalty programmes, and same-day delivery logistics. The legal retail network has scaled to over 4,000 stores across Canada but the consumer software layer remains fragmented. Category-killer software in cannabis retail is still available to the right founder.

03

Export and global expansion

Canadian LPs and technology companies positioned for medical cannabis export into Germany, Australia, and other liberalising markets. Regulatory-arbitrage plays that leverage Canada's existing supply base and GMP certifications to access premium-priced international markets before domestic oversupply further compresses margins.
